What sets a professional trader apart from others?

Where and how a professional trader opens a trade?

While millions of people participate in forex trading, only a few achieve success. Success is influenced by
factors like theoretical and practical knowledge, as well as risk management. However, one characteristic that
distinguishes a professional trader is discipline, according to Trader.org. This quality enables them to adhere
to the rules they establish based on their theoretical knowledge and practical experience, leading to consistent success.
